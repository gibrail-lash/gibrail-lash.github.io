Process
Interpret

I believe the most critical step in the design process is to define - and comprehend - the problem. After all, what use is a solution if it doesnt actually solve the problem. To fully understand a problem, I delve into questions, related and unrelated, that may need to be asked during any stage of the project. This allows myself to observe the problem from many angles to determine characteristics such as design constraints and functional requirements.

Ideate

Once the problem is fully defined, I proceed to formulate possible solutions. As a designer, it is especially important to consider the end user into the design, as I believe usability is just as, if not more, important as functionality.
